Job Responsibilities

Working with Hot Beans means monitoring websites and webpages, as well as using coding techniques to create your own websites and webpages. You will be asked to meet client requirements and reach greatness with each and every part of your work.

Job Qualifications

You will need skills in:

-Visual Design.

-UX (User Experience).

-SEO (Search Engine Optimisation), marketing and social media.

-Being experienced with coding and being able to use coding software (HTML, Javascript, CSS).

-To be able to use Coding knowledge to be able to create websites, either from scratch or by adapting existing websites.

-Web Programming Skills, Verbal Communication.

Times of work may vary, but they are usually 30-40 hours a week, working 9:00am to 5:30pm, Monday to Friday.

Salaries will be between £18,000 and £26,000, depending on what level of development you are working on.
