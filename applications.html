Job Description

In order to apply for Hot Beans, you must have a love for Web design and Devloping! You will be responsible for the help of creating webpages and websites! This will mean using HTML, CSS and Javascript! But if you don't understand coding, don't panic because our staff will be more than willing to teach you the basics of coding, and some will teach you the advanced coding!

You will have to either continue with existing projects to help other junior developers, or you will start a new project form scratch! You will be working as a team throughout your time with Hot Beans so if you ever need help, you can always ask your team or even a member of staff!

As a web designer you must:

Have skills in programming and graphics,

Be creative and use your imagination,

Be able to adapt to new techniques,

Have good communication skills,

Keep up to date with advances in computer technology and acknowledge how this affects the business!
